Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about KARSAKAH AI

Karsakah AI is a smart agriculture platform that helps farmers make better farming decisions using artificial intelligence, weather data, and agricultural insights. It provides tools such as crop disease detection, crop recommendations, irrigation advice, and market price information.

Farmers can upload an image of a crop leaf or plant. Our AI model analyzes the image and identifies possible diseases affecting the crop. The system then suggests recommended treatments and preventive measures.

Karsakah AI provides data-driven insights such as weather forecasts, soil-based crop recommendations, and irrigation guidance. These insights help farmers make informed decisions that can improve crop productivity and reduce losses.

  • Web technologies for the platform
  • Weather and agriculture data APIs
